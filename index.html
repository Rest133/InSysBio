InSysBio

a private research organization focused on application of system-pharmacology modeling to different stages of drug research and development.

Our mission

At InSysBio our mission is to provide QSP-modeling to world pharma to assist in efficient drug development thus bringing hope to patients.

«Building models – Bringing hope»

Our vision

Our vision is to establish QSP-modeling as an essential part of pharmaceutical industry in order to dramatically reduce risks and time consumption of drug development cycle.

Values

Commitment to patients

Day-by-day we do our best to take steps in global diversified drug development process finally aimed at delivery medicines to patients. We perform our function to make the process faster to maximize patients’ life quality.

Partners support

We provide individual approach towards customers’ needs and decrease risks of their time and funds loss. Our approach of the complete annotation of our models allows the partner to effortlessly reproduce and operate the model for further purposes.

Creative atmosphere

We create favourable atmosphere for self-development of researchers specializing in quantitative pharmacology and mathematical modeling in biology and medicine.

Educational assistance to academic entities

We provide comprehensive support to academic entities from giving the courses on mathematical modeling in universities to provision of free usage of our softs to facilitate their research activity.

QSP modeling popularization

We develop and popularize mathematical modeling in the sphere of biology and medicine. Our researchers have published a number of scientific reports,regularly present at major international conferences.
