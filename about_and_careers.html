About

InSysBio (formerly Institute for Systems Biology Moscow and Institute for Systems Biology SPb) was founded in 2004.

Our main activity is the application of mathematical modeling for pharmacology, personal medicine, and biotechnology. The InSysBio innovative approach has become an integral part of the drug development process implemented by our strategic partners.

Today, InSysBio has more than 30 qualified specialists, collaborates with leading pharmacological companies, works with students and postgraduates, has published a number of scientific reports, and has presented at major international conferences.

In addition, our business is not limited to only mathematical modeling; InSysBio develops software for specialists in systems pharmacology and systems biology. We are always ready to help in solving actual complex problems.

Careers

InSysBio is looking for researchers who are experienced in the area of mathematical modeling. InSysBio is one of the leaders in systems pharmacology modeling. We provide innovative modeling solutions to our biopharmaceutical clients. Our experts are involved in projects in different therapeutic areas, such as oncology, metabolic diseases, inflammatory disease, neurodegenerative diseases, etc. In our company, we use a variety available methods for mathematical modeling and develop our own methods. InSysBio is the best way to apply your knowledge and skills for the benefit of drug discovery and development.

InSysBio is looking for talented students with biological/medical/mathematical backgrounds to participate in different interesting projects in such areas as type 2 diabetes mellitus, cancer, and chronic kidney disease, etc. Our company is one of the best ways to start your career in the field of systems pharmacology modeling.
