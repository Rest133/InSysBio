Research

InSysBio is specialized in the development and application of methods of mathematical modeling for solving actual problems arising in pharmacology and biomedicine. The biological objects of interest are complex systems, and their mathematical analysis is associated with complicated computational methods and approaches. The choice of theoretical technique for each particular case depends on the type of questions to answer as well as the features of the investigated system.


Nowadays, mathematical modeling for biology and medicine is a rapidly developing academic and applied research area. This can be easily seen from high scientific publishing activity in the field and the increase of drug development projects involving modeling research. That is why we have focused not only on the application of known techniques for modeling, but also on the development of new methods and approaches for better results. We have all the necessary experience and scientific background for such ambitious objectives. Despite the number of possible research fields, we selected several areas that are the priorities of our work.
